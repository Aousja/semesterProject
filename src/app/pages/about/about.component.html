Our Story | ہماری کہانی

In the heart of the bustling city, Zaiqa Darbar stood tall, its intricate architecture a testament to the rich cultural heritage of the land. The aroma of exotic spices and freshly brewed coffee wafted through the air, enticing passersby to enter its grand doors. As night fell, the Darbar transformed into a vibrant hub of activity, with musicians, dancers, and storytellers gathering to regale audiences with tales of old.
